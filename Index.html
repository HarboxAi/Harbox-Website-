Australian Visa Pathways

15 visa categories · Educational information only · Not immigration advice

Subclass 189Permanent
Skilled Independent
Points-tested visa for skilled workers. No employer or state sponsor required.
Subclass 190Permanent
Skilled Nominated
Points-tested visa with state or territory nomination. Adds a published 5-point allocation.
Subclass 491Provisional
Skilled Work Regional
Provisional visa for skilled workers nominated to live and work in regional Australia.
Subclass 482Temporary
Skills in Demand
Employer-sponsored temporary visa for workers in occupations in demand in Australia.
Subclass 186Permanent
Employer Nomination Scheme
Permanent visa for skilled workers nominated by an approved Australian employer.
Subclass 494Provisional
Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional
Employer-sponsored provisional visa for skilled workers in regional Australia.
Subclass 485Temporary
Temporary Graduate
Allows recent international graduates to live and work in Australia after studies.
Subclass 191Permanent
Permanent Residence (Regional)
Permanent visa for holders of certain provisional visas who have lived and worked regionally.
Subclass 500Temporary
Student
Allows international students to study full-time in a registered course in Australia.
Subclass 417Temporary
Working Holiday
For young adults from eligible countries to holiday and work in Australia.
Subclass 462Temporary
Work and Holiday
Similar to Working Holiday but for citizens of different eligible countries.
Subclass 309/100Perm/Temp
Partner (Offshore)
For partners of Australian citizens or permanent residents applying from outside Australia.
Subclass 820/801Perm/Temp
Partner (Onshore)
For partners of Australian citizens or permanent residents already in Australia.
Subclass 300Temporary
Prospective Marriage
Allows you to travel to Australia to marry your prospective Australian citizen or PR spouse.
Subclass 600Temporary
Visitor — Tourist Stream
For people visiting Australia for tourism, visiting family, or business visitor activities.

Understanding your visa situation shouldn't be this hard

Millions want to build a new life in Australia — but the visa process is complex, time-consuming, and often difficult to understand without professional help. Most people don't know where to start.

HARBOX changes that. We organise publicly available government information and put it directly in your hands — clearly, simply, and affordably.
